## Product Overview

MaestroAgent is an enterprise organizational intelligence platform. It ingests
execution signals from connected tools (GitHub, Jira, Slack, Confluence, Gmail,
Salesforce), builds an Organizational Execution Model (OEM), and surfaces
decisions, risks, and predictions to executives through a Bumble-inspired
interface: bold, confident, one-card-at-a-time.

**The ambient layer** (CEO's vision, July 2026): Maestro is not just an app — it
is an organizational operating layer. Intelligence is centralized; delivery is
everywhere. The browser extension appears on Gmail, GitHub, Jira, Slack, Zoom,
Salesforce, Notion, and Figma with a 4-part card: Situation → Insight → Evidence
→ Action. The golden rule: never interrupt — only arrive when intelligence
changes a decision.

## The Ambient Layer — Organizational Cognition That Appears

The ambient layer is not a feature. It is the delivery mechanism for everything
Maestro knows. Intelligence is centralized in the OEM; delivery is everywhere
work happens.

### The 4-Part Whisper Card

Every ambient card contains exactly four parts — no exceptions:

1. **Situation** — what the user is doing (e.g. 'Preparing for meeting with Globex')
2. **Insight** — what Maestro noticed (e.g. 'Engineering already promised: Deliver SSO by 2024-12-15')
3. **Evidence** — where this came from (list of {source, date, text})
4. **Action** — what to do next (e.g. 'View commitment' → opens the customer surface)

```
  Evidence:  1 item(s)
  Action:    View commitment (type: open_in_maestro)
  Priority:  high
  Confidence: 100%

Whisper 2:
  Situation: Preparing for meeting with Globex
  Insight:   Globex previously decided: renewed
  Evidence:  2 item(s)
  Action:    View prior decision (type: open_in_maestro)
  Priority:  medium
  Confidence: 100%

Whisper 3:
  Situation: Preparing for meeting with Globex
  Insight:   Past evidence: Objection raised by Initech: pricing
  Evidence:  1 item(s)
  Action:    View evidence (type: open_in_maestro)
  Priority:  low
  Confidence: 32%

```

### The Golden Rule

> Never interrupt. Only arrive when intelligence changes a decision.

- Only `priority='high'` whispers auto-show (commitments, objections, broken commitments)
- Same insight doesn't show twice in 60 seconds
- 5-second auto-dismiss (unless hovered)
- Outcome tracking: POST /api/oem/whisper/outcome records acted/ignored/overrode
