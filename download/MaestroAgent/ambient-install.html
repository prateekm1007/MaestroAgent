Maestro Ambient Overlay

Surface organizational judgment on any page — without opening Maestro.

Privacy by design. The overlay only reads the URL domain and page title. It never inspects page content, email bodies, or keystrokes. It activates only when you click the bookmarklet — Maestro never auto-activates.

Install the bookmarklet

1 Configure your Maestro host:

2 Drag this link to your bookmarks bar:
Maestro Whisper
3 Click the bookmarklet on any page.
When you're in Gmail, Calendar, GitHub, Jira, Slack, or Zoom, click "Maestro Whisper" in your bookmarks bar. A small panel appears in the corner with organizational judgment relevant to your current context.

What it does

When you click the bookmarklet, Maestro:

  • Detects your context — which app you're in (Gmail, Calendar, GitHub, etc.)
  • Infers your intent — preparing for a negotiation? reviewing code? resolving an incident?
  • Surfaces a whisper — what the organization knows that's relevant right now
  • Shows interrupts — events that warrant your attention, filtered by priority
  • Displays the pulse — a one-line summary of the organization's current state

The panel auto-dismisses after 15 seconds. If Maestro has nothing to add, it says so and disappears in 2 seconds.

How it works

The bookmarklet calls /api/oem/ambient with only three pieces of information:

  • active_app — which app you're in (inferred from the URL domain)
  • calendar_title — the page title (only useful on Calendar)
  • url_context — the URL domain (not the full path)

No page content is read. No keystrokes are logged. No email bodies are inspected. The overlay appears only when you explicitly click the bookmarklet.
